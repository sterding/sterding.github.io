Nucleosome positioning prediction

ontoCAT

Ontology parsing

BHC

Bayesian Hierarchical Clustering

iSeq

Bayesian Hierarchical Modeling of ChIP-seq Data Through Hidden Ising Models

URL: http://bioconductor.org/news/bioc_2_7_release/

btw, other interesting ones in BioC 2.6:

New sequence analysis tools address infrastructure (GenomicRanges, Rsamtools, girafe); ChIP-seq (BayesPeak, CSAR, PICS); digital gene expression and RNA-seq (DESeq, goseq, segmentSeq); and motif discovery (MotIV, rGADEM).
