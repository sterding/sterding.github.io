Hi, I'm Xianjun Dong. I work on the human genome as a computer geek.

While hiking to the peak of Science, I enjoy a lot the beauty lying between the elegance of the human genome and the complexity of human brain, where I wish to fit in my two bits as a computer geek.

I act as the Director of Genomics and Bioinformatics Hub in ARCND and of Computational Neuroscience in Dr. Scherzer's Neurogenomics Laboratory in Brigham and Women's Hospital, an Assistant Professor of Neurology at Harvard Medical School. I'm recently funded for studying Parkinson's disease, and looking to bring bioinformatics to more collaborative research in brain diseases.
